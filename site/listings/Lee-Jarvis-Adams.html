Lee-Jarvis-Adams House

Spacious c.1794 Federal situated in the heart of Castine village in a National Historic District with harbor views. Located in the commercial district for either residential or commercial options. Five bedrooms and a home “theater”, four fireplaces, unfinished ell over the two car garage, large attic with two rooms. Historic architectural features including double staircase, dentil moldings, hardwood floors. $750,000
